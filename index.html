This one is my recipe, we will start on how to make sourdough starter first.

Sourdough Starter Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup water (filtered or dechlorinated)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the whole wheat flour and water until well combined.
  2. Cover the bowl with a clean cloth and let it sit at room temperature for 24 hours.
  3. After 24 hours, you should see some bubbles forming. This indicates that fermentation has started.
  4. Discard half of the starter and feed it with 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour and 1/2 cup of water. Mix well.
  5. Repeat this feeding process every 24 hours for about 5-7 days, until the starter is bubbly and has a pleasant sour smell.

Your sourdough starter is now ready to use for baking delicious bread!
