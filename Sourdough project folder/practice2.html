Fella's Classic Foccacia

Who doesn't love a good,warm fluffy foccacia?

Foccacia is a popular Italian flatbread known for its airy texture and crispy crust. It's often topped with olive oil, herbs, and various ingredients like tomatoes, olives, and onions. Perfect for sandwiches or as a side to soups and salads, foccacia is a versatile and delicious bread that can be enjoyed in many ways.

recipe for my simple Foccacia

ingredients

  • 100 g Sourdough starter
  • 350 g filtered water
  • 500 g all-purpose flour
  • 10 g salt
  • 15-20 g olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • Toppings: cherry tomatoes, olives, rosemary, caramelized onions,,anything you wish

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sourdough starter and water. Stir until the starter is dissolved.
  2. Add the flour, olive oil and salt to the bowl. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Transfer the dough to a lightly oiled baking pan. Perform a series of stretch and folds every 30 mn for 2 hours.
  5. Cover the pan with a damp cloth and let the dough rise for 4 hours.
  6. Put in the fridge for cold fermentation overnight.
  7. the next day,take it out of the fridge, grease a baking pan and flip the dough in it cover and let it rest for 2 hours
  8. Preheat your oven to 220Â°C.
  9. Before baking, use your fingers to create dimples all over the surface of the dough. Drizzle with olive oil and add your desired toppings.
  10. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the foccacia is golden brown and cooked through.
  11. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
